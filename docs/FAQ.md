# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is StacksYield Pro?
StacksYield Pro is a decentralized yield aggregator built on the Stacks blockchain. It allows users to deposit STX and earn yields through various vault strategies.

### Is StacksYield Pro safe to use?
StacksYield Pro smart contracts are designed with security in mind. However, as with any DeFi protocol, there are inherent risks. Always do your own research and only invest what you can afford to lose.

### What wallet do I need?
You need a Stacks-compatible wallet like Leather (formerly Hiro Wallet) or Xverse to interact with StacksYield Pro.

## Deposits & Withdrawals

### How do I make a deposit?
1. Connect your wallet
2. Select a vault
3. Enter the amount to deposit
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet

### Is there a minimum deposit?
Minimum deposits vary by vault. Check the specific vault details for requirements.

### How long does a deposit take?
Deposits are confirmed once the transaction is included in a Stacks block, typically within 10-30 minutes.

### Can I withdraw anytime?
Yes, most vaults allow instant withdrawals. Some may have a brief unlock period.

## Rewards

### How are rewards calculated?
Rewards are calculated based on your share of the vault's total deposits and the vault's APY.

### When can I claim rewards?
Rewards can be claimed at any time. They accumulate automatically.

### Are rewards auto-compounded?
This depends on the vault strategy. Some vaults auto-compound while others require manual claiming.

## Referral Program

### How does the referral program work?
Share your unique referral link. When someone deposits using your link, you earn a percentage of their rewards.

### How do I get my referral link?
Connect your wallet and navigate to the Referral section to get your unique link.

## Technical

### What blockchain does StacksYield Pro use?
StacksYield Pro is built on Stacks, a Layer 2 Bitcoin blockchain.

### Are the smart contracts audited?
Our contracts undergo internal reviews and community audits. See SECURITY.md for details.

## WalletConnect

### How do I connect with mobile wallet?
1. Click "Connect Wallet"
2. Select "WalletConnect" option
3. Scan the QR code with Xverse or Leather mobile app
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

### Why isn't the QR code scanning?
Use the scanner built into your wallet app, not your phone's camera app. The `wc:` protocol requires the wallet's scanner.
