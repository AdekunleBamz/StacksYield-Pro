# Glossary

## DeFi Terms

### APY (Annual Percentage Yield)
The effective annual rate of return taking into account the effect of compounding interest.

### APR (Annual Percentage Rate)
The annual rate of return without compounding.

### Liquidity
The ease with which an asset can be converted to another asset without affecting its market price.

### Yield Farming
The practice of staking or lending crypto assets to generate returns.

### TVL (Total Value Locked)
The total value of assets deposited in a DeFi protocol.

### Smart Contract
Self-executing code stored on a blockchain that automatically enforces rules and agreements.

### Gas Fees
Transaction fees paid to process operations on a blockchain.

### Vault
A smart contract that manages deposits and executes yield-generating strategies.

### Staking
Locking up cryptocurrency to support blockchain operations and earn rewards.

## Stacks-Specific Terms

### STX
The native token of the Stacks blockchain.

### Clarity
The smart contract programming language used on Stacks.

### Proof of Transfer (PoX)
Stacks' consensus mechanism that connects to Bitcoin.

### Stacking
The Stacks-specific term for staking STX to earn Bitcoin rewards.

### Principal
An address or contract identifier on the Stacks blockchain.

## StacksYield Pro Terms

### Vault Strategy
The specific approach a vault uses to generate yield.

### Reward Multiplier
Bonus applied to rewards based on certain conditions.

### Referral Bonus
Percentage of rewards earned from referred users.

### Deposit Receipt
Proof of deposit in a vault, used to track user positions.

### Pending Rewards
Accumulated rewards that haven't been claimed yet.
