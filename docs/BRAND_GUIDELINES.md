# Brand Guidelines

## StacksYield Pro Brand Assets

This document outlines the brand guidelines for StacksYield Pro.

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
Use the primary logo on light backgrounds.

### Logo Variations
- Full logo with text
- Icon only
- Horizontal layout
- Vertical layout

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "S" in StacksYield.

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 120px width minimum
- Print: 1 inch width minimum

## Colors

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Blue**: #3B82F6
- **Primary Dark**: #1E40AF

### Secondary Colors
- **Success Green**: #10B981
- **Warning Yellow**: #F59E0B
- **Error Red**: #EF4444

### Neutral Colors
- **Gray 900**: #111827
- **Gray 700**: #374151
- **Gray 500**: #6B7280
- **Gray 300**: #D1D5DB
- **Gray 100**: #F3F4F6

## Typography

### Primary Font
Inter - Used for headings and body text

### Font Weights
- Regular (400): Body text
- Medium (500): Subheadings
- Semibold (600): Headings
- Bold (700): Emphasis

## Tone of Voice

- Professional yet approachable
- Clear and concise
- Confident but not arrogant
- Educational and helpful

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use official brand colors
- Maintain logo proportions
- Follow clear space guidelines

### Don't
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Use unapproved colors
- Add effects to the logo
