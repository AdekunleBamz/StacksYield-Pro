## Setting Up Your Wallet

Before using StacksYield Pro, you need a Stacks wallet:

### Recommended Wallets

1. **Leather Wallet** (formerly Hiro Wallet)
   - Download from: https://leather.io
   - Available as browser extension

2. **Xverse**
   - Download from: https://xverse.app
   - Available on mobile and browser

### Creating a Wallet

1. Install your preferred wallet
2. Create a new wallet
3. **IMPORTANT**: Securely save your seed phrase
4. Fund your wallet with STX

## Connecting to StacksYield Pro

1. Visit the StacksYield Pro application
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the header
3. Select your wallet provider
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

### Mobile Wallets (WalletConnect)

If you connect from a mobile wallet (Leather or Xverse):

1. Click "Connect Wallet" to open the QR modal
2. Use your wallet’s built-in WalletConnect scanner to scan the QR code
3. If your phone camera doesn’t recognize the QR, use the "Open on phone" link
4. Approve the session in your wallet to complete the connection

## Understanding Vaults

Vaults are smart contracts that manage your deposits and generate yield.

### Vault Information

- **APY**: Annual Percentage Yield
- **TVL**: Total Value Locked
- **Min Deposit**: Minimum deposit required
- **Strategy**: How the vault generates yield

## Making Your First Deposit

1. Browse available vaults
2. Select a vault that matches your goals
3. Enter the amount to deposit
4. Click "Deposit"
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
6. Wait for blockchain confirmation

## Claiming Rewards

1. Go to your Dashboard
2. View your accumulated rewards
3. Click "Claim Rewards"
4. Confirm the transaction
5. Rewards are sent to your wallet

## Withdrawing Funds

1. Navigate to your Dashboard
2. Select the vault to withdraw from
3. Enter the withdrawal amount
4. Click "Withdraw"
5. Confirm the transaction
6. Funds return to your wallet

## Tips for Success

- Start with a small test deposit
- Diversify across multiple vaults
- Regularly claim and compound rewards
- Keep your wallet software updated
- Never share your seed phrase
